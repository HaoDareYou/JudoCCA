About Judo Club

Our Story

Founded in 2015, NYP Judo Club began when a handful of determined students rolled out their first tatami mats in 2015. What started as late-night training sessions between classes grew into an official dojo where technique and camaraderie collide. We've stayed true to our scrappy beginnings - valuing grit over gear, and community over championships (though we've collected plenty of those too). Every bow, breakfall, and bout carries forward the founders' vision: judo for all, excellence for those who earn it.

Coaches & Leaders

Coach Azfar

Head Coach — Coach Azfar has extensive experience teaching judo to adults in various settings. He has also previously coached judo at BJJ, Krav Maga, and MMA gyms.

Assistant Coach Jiayao

Assistant Coach Jiayao — Jiayao is a highly detail-oriented instructor with exceptional technical precision. His methodical approach and deep understanding of judo mechanics allow him to refine techniques for both competitors and recreational practitioners.

Captain James Ng

Captain James — With a commanding presence and deep technical mastery, James sets the standard for excellence in both practice and competition. His ability to analyse and elevate team performance—while leading by example—makes him an indispensable pillar of our judo community.
